Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change my learning language?

You can change your learning language in your Profile settings:

  1. Open the app and go to your Profile tab
  2. Tap on "Learning Language"
  3. Select your new target language
  4. Your progress will be saved separately for each language
Why isn't my microphone working during conversation practice?

Common microphone issues and solutions:

  • Permissions: Ensure Kielo has microphone access in your device settings
  • Other apps: Close other apps that might be using the microphone
  • Hardware: Test your microphone with another app to ensure it's working
  • Network: Poor internet connection can affect voice recognition
  • Restart: Close and reopen the app, or restart your device
How do I cancel my premium subscription?

To cancel your subscription:

  1. Go to Profile ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Subscription
  2. Select "Manage Subscription"
  3. Choose "Cancel Subscription"
  4. Confirm cancellation

EU Consumer Rights: You have 14 days from purchase to request a full refund. Contact connect+billing@kielo.app for refund requests.

How is my conversation data used and stored?

Your conversation practice data:

  • Voice recordings: Processed in real-time for speech recognition, then deleted within 24-48 hours
  • Conversation transcripts: Stored to provide your learning history and improve our AI
  • Third-party processing: Voice data is processed by our AI partners but not stored long-term
  • Your control: You can delete conversation history anytime in Privacy settings

For complete details, see our Privacy Policy.

How do I delete my account and data?

To permanently delete your account:

  1. Go to Profile ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Privacy
  2. Select "Delete Account"
  3. Read the deletion information carefully
  4. Confirm deletion by typing your email address
  5. Your data will be permanently removed within 30 days

Note: This action cannot be undone. All your learning progress, conversations, and account data will be permanently deleted.

What are my rights under GDPR?

As an EU data subject, you have these rights:

  • Right to be informed: Clear information about how we use your data
  • Right of access: Get a copy of your personal data
  • Right to rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to restrict processing: Limit how we process your data
  • Right to data portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to object: Object to certain types of data processing

Contact connect+privacy@kielo.app to exercise any of these rights.
