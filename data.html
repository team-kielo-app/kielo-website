Data Processing Agreement

Last Updated: August 11, 2025

GDPR Article 28 Compliance: This Data Processing Agreement (DPA) governs the processing of personal data by Kielo App and complies with Article 28 of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Parties and Definitions

Data Controller: The User of Kielo App services

Data Processor: Kielo App

Sub-processors: Third-party service providers engaged by Kielo App

Processing: Any operation performed on personal data, as defined in GDPR Article 4(2)

Personal Data: Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person, as defined in GDPR Article 4(1)

2. Scope and Duration

This DPA applies to the processing of personal data by Kielo App on behalf of users in the context of providing language learning services. The agreement remains in effect for the duration of the service relationship and governs all personal data processed during this period.

3. Nature and Purpose of Processing

Kielo App processes personal data for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: Delivering language learning services, managing user accounts, and providing customer support
  • Personalization: Customizing learning experiences based on user progress and preferences
  • Analytics: Improving service quality and developing new features through anonymized usage analytics
  • Communication: Sending service-related notifications and updates
  • Security: Ensuring platform security and preventing fraudulent activities

5. Processing Instructions

Kielo App will process personal data only:

  • For the purposes outlined in this DPA and our Privacy Policy
  • In accordance with documented user instructions through the app interface
  • To comply with applicable EU or Member State law requirements
  • Following the data minimization principle - processing only necessary data

Reference: GDPR Article 28(3)(a)

6. Confidentiality and Staff Training

Kielo App ensures that:

  • All personnel with access to personal data are bound by confidentiality obligations
  • Staff receive regular GDPR and data protection training
  • Access to personal data is granted on a need-to-know basis
  • Background checks are performed for personnel handling sensitive data
  • Confidentiality obligations survive termination of employment

Reference: GDPR Article 28(3)(b)

7. Security Measures

Technical and Organizational Security Measures

Kielo App implements appropriate security measures including:

  • Encryption: AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit
  • Access Controls: Multi-factor authentication, role-based access
  • Network Security: Firewalls, intrusion detection systems
  • Data Backup: Regular encrypted backups with geographic separation
  • Incident Response: 24/7 monitoring and response procedures
  • Regular Testing: Penetration testing and vulnerability assessments

Reference: GDPR Article 32

9. Data Subject Rights

Kielo App assists users in responding to data subject rights requests by:

  • Access Requests: Providing user data exports within 30 days
  • Rectification: Enabling users to update personal information
  • Erasure: Account deletion functionality with data purging
  • Portability: Machine-readable data export capabilities
  • Restriction: Temporary processing limitations upon request
  • Objection: Opt-out mechanisms for marketing and analytics

Contact connect+privacy@kielo.app for assistance with data subject requests.

Reference: GDPR Articles 15-22

10. Data Breach Notification

In case of a personal data breach, Kielo App will:

  • Notify affected users within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach
  • Provide details about the nature of the breach and affected data
  • Describe measures taken to address the breach
  • Recommend actions for users to protect themselves
  • Assist with regulatory notifications as required

Reference: GDPR Article 33-34

11. Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA)

Kielo App conducts DPIAs for high-risk processing activities, including:

  • AI-powered voice processing and analysis
  • Automated decision-making for learning recommendations
  • Large-scale behavioral analytics

DPIA results are available upon request for transparency purposes.

12. International Data Transfers

When transferring personal data outside the EEA, Kielo App ensures adequate protection through:

  • Adequacy Decisions: Transfers to countries with adequate protection levels
  • Standard Contractual Clauses: EU-approved contractual protections
  • Binding Corporate Rules: For intra-group transfers where applicable
  • Derogations: Only for specific situations outlined in GDPR Article 49

Reference: GDPR Chapter V

13. Data Retention and Deletion

Personal data retention periods:

  • Account Data: Retained until account deletion + 30 days for security
  • Learning Progress: Deleted immediately upon account deletion
  • Conversation Data: Retained for 2 years or until account deletion
  • Voice Recordings: Deleted within 24-48 hours after processing
  • Support Tickets: Retained for 3 years for quality assurance
  • Legal Hold: Data may be retained longer for legal compliance

Automated deletion processes ensure timely data removal according to these schedules.

14. Audit and Compliance

Kielo App provides audit capabilities including:

  • Annual third-party security audits and certifications
  • Access to relevant compliance documentation
  • Regular internal privacy assessments
  • Processing activity logs and audit trails
  • Cooperation with supervisory authority investigations

Audit requests should be submitted to connect+legal@kielo.app with reasonable advance notice.

Reference: GDPR Article 28(3)(h)

15. Termination of Processing

Upon termination of the service relationship, Kielo App will:

  • Cease all processing of personal data
  • Delete or return personal data as instructed by the user
  • Provide confirmation of data deletion within 30 days
  • Delete existing copies unless retention is required by law
  • Ensure sub-processors also delete or return personal data

16. Liability and Indemnification

Each party is liable for damages caused by its own GDPR violations. Kielo App maintains appropriate insurance coverage for data protection liabilities and will indemnify users for damages resulting from our processing violations, subject to applicable law limitations.

17. Governing Law and Dispute Resolution

This DPA is governed by EU law and the laws of Finland. Disputes will be resolved through:

  • Direct negotiation between the parties
  • Mediation services if negotiation fails
  • Jurisdiction of Finnish courts
  • Compliance with supervisory authority decisions

18. Updates to this Agreement

This DPA may be updated to reflect:

  • Changes in applicable data protection laws
  • Updates to our processing activities
  • New sub-processor arrangements
  • Enhanced security measures

Material changes will be communicated 30 days in advance via email notification.
